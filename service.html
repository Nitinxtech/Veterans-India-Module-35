Social and Political Awareness Wing

Empowering veterans, youth, and citizens with civic awareness, democratic values, and active participation.

About the Wing

The Social and Political Awareness Wing of Veterans India works to instill civic responsibility, political literacy, and democratic values among citizens, focusing on youth and students.

  • Vision: Create an informed and engaged citizenry that actively participates in democracy.
  • Mission: Empower youth through education, campaigns, and leadership programs.
  • Approach: Education, awareness campaigns, partnerships, and community engagement.

Our Objectives

Educational Initiatives

Civic education in schools & colleges, workshops on democracy, governance, and electoral processes.

Public Campaigns

Awareness campaigns on rights & duties using social and traditional media for wide reach.

Youth Engagement

Forums, discussions, youth parliaments, and leadership development programs.

Collaboration

Partnerships with NGOs, educational institutions, media, and government bodies.

Key Activities

Workshops & Seminars

Conducting political literacy programs, democratic education, and awareness workshops.

Awareness Campaigns

Spreading knowledge on voting rights, governance, and responsibilities through media campaigns.

Community Engagement

Engaging local communities with dialogues, youth parliaments, and patriotic events.

Important Dates

  • ✔ Republic Day (Jan 26) – Awareness campaigns on democratic values.
  • ✔ National Voters’ Day (Jan 25) – Voter education & participation programs.
  • ✔ Constitution Day (Nov 26) – Events on constitutional values.
  • ✔ Independence Day (Aug 15) – Patriotism & civic responsibility programs.
  • ✔ International Day of Democracy (Sept 15) – Political participation events.
