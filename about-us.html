About Veterans India

Veterans India, established in 2015 under the visionary leadership of Dr. BK Mishra, stands as a national, apolitical, and patriotic organization dedicated to fostering nationalism and patriotism among citizens.

Our Mission Our Team

Our Mission & Vision

Dedicated to Nation Building

Our Mission

To foster nationalism and patriotism among citizens, promote unity, integrity, and service to the nation, and provide a platform for committed individuals and organizations to show their respect and gratitude towards the true heroes of our nation.

Our Vision

To create a society where the valor, bravery, and supreme sacrifices of our soldiers are honored, and to work for the welfare of veterans, their families, and the nation at large.

Key Achievements

  • Established in 2015
  • National level organization with multiple specialized wings
  • Patron support from distinguished veterans and leaders
  • Comprehensive support for veterans, families, and society
  • Active in social, cultural, legal, environmental, and empowerment initiatives
