About Veterans India

Dedicated to Nation Building

Our Mission

Veterans India stands as a national, apolitical, and patriotic organization dedicated to fostering nationalism and patriotism among citizens. We work tirelessly to promote the values of unity, integrity, and service to the nation.

Our Vision

To create a platform where committed individuals and organizations can show their respect and gratitude towards the true heroes of our nation - our brave soldiers who exhibited supreme valor and bravery to defend our nation's unity and integrity.

Key Achievements

  • Established in 2015
  • National level organization
  • Multiple specialized wings
  • Patron support from distinguished veterans

Our Wings & Services

Comprehensive Support for Veterans and Society

Martyr Soldier's Family & Disabled Soldier's Welfare

Supporting families of martyrs and disabled soldiers with comprehensive welfare programs.

Sports Wing

Promoting sports and physical fitness among veterans and youth for nation building.

Nari Sena & Women Empowerment

Empowering women and promoting gender equality through various initiatives.

Employment & Skill Development

Providing employment opportunities and skill development programs for veterans.

Legal Wing

Providing legal assistance and support to veterans and their families.

Environmental Wing

Working towards environmental conservation and sustainable development.
